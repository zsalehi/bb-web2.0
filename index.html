About BlockBusters

A trivia competition like none other

The BlockBusters Organization was conceived ten years ago during the Summer of 2012 by a group of youth with the goal of creating a platform that allowed our communities to continuously learn and fill the void that consisted of a lack of enthusiasm in gaining knowledge by our youth. Our vision was to embody the message from our Holy Prophet (as) to "Seek knowledge from the cradle to the grave", by creating an engaging way for the members of our communities to learn from each other. Since then, the BlockBusters Trivia Competition has become the largest of its kind in the Toronto Shi'a Community.
